El Nido

It is renowned for its white sandy shores, vibrant coral reefs, and its role as the entrance to the Bacuit archipelago, which consists of a collection of islands characterized by towering karst cliffs.

Duli Beach

When you set foot on Duli Beach in northern Palawan, you’ll be greeted by a panorama of pristine white sands, an abundance of palm trees, and crystal clear waters.

Coron

Often viewed as the more serene and relaxed counterpart to El Nido, another well-known destination in Palawan, it is celebrated for its breathtaking waters and landscapes.
